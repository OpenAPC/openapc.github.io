The [VSB - Technical University of Ostrava](https://www.vsb.cz/en) has updated its APC expenditures, the latest contribution provides data for the 2024 period.

The [VSB Central Library](https://knihovna.vsb.cz/en) collects data on institutional APCs expenditures and kindly provides that data to OpenAPC.

Contact Person is [Lenka Votýpková](mailto:lenka.votypkova@vsb.cz).

## Cost data



The new data set covers publication fees for 168 articles, total expenditure amounts to 240,788€ and the average fee is 1,433€.
