The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University Medical Center Hamburg-Eppendorf](https://www.uke.de/forschung/open-science/publikationsfoerderung/index.html) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Sonja Clauditz](mailto:s.clauditz@uke.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 351 articles (130 unrelated to any agreement, 221 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 906,391€ and the average fee is 2,582€.
