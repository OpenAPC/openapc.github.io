The [Technische Universität München (TUM)](https://www.tum.de/en/) has updated its expenditures, providing data on APCs and on articles published under the German DEAL agreements with Wiley, Springer Nature and Elsevier for the 2024 period.

The [TUM University Library](http://www.ub.tum.de/en) is in charge of the [University's Open Access Publishing Fund](https://www.ub.tum.de/en/publishing-fund), which received support by the DFG under its [Open-Access Publishing Programme](https://www.dfg.de/en/research_funding/programmes/infrastructure/lis/open_access/infrastructure_funding/index.html#4) until 2019.

Contact person is [Gregor Reiss](mailto:open-access@ub.tum.de).

### Cost Data



The new data covers publication fees for 202 articles. Total expenditure amounts to 252,310€ and the average fee is 1,249€. Please note that articles published under the transformative DEAL agreements are not included in this list, as they are aggregated in OpenAPC’s [transformative agreements data set](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/tree/master/data/transformative_agreements).
