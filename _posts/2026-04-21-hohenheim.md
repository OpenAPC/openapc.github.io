The [University of Hohenheim](https://www.uni-hohenheim.de/en/english) has updated its expenditures, the latest contribution provides APC, BCP and TA data for the 2025 period.

Data is provided by the [Communication, Information and Media Center (KIM)](https://kim.uni-hohenheim.de/en), which manages services related to [Open Access](https://kim.uni-hohenheim.de/en/openaccess).

Contact person is [Isabo Gerstner](mailto:openaccess@uni-hohenheim.de).


## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 116 articles (55 unrelated to any agreement, 61 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 244,184€ and the average fee is 2,105€.

The following table provides an overview. Please note that BPCs are not included in this list, as they are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) dataset
