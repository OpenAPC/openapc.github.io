The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Brandenburg University of Technology Cottbus-Senftenberg](https://www.b-tu.de/en/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Charlotte Meixner](mailto:openaccess@b-tu.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 34 articles (25 unrelated to any agreement, 9 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 79,767€ and the average fee is 2,346€.
