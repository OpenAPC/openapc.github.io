[Lucerne University of Applied Sciences and Arts](https://www.hslu.ch/en/) (Hochschule Luzern, HSLU) has updated its APC expenditures, providing data for the 2022 period.

Contact Person is [Simone Rosenkranz](mailto:Simone.Rosenkranz@zhbluzern.ch).

## Cost data



The new dataset covers publication fees for 13 articles. Total expenditure amounts to 19 415€ and the average fee is 1 493€. The data also contained one BPC record which is not listed here.

|Frontiers Media SA                                     |        6|             10430|          1738|
