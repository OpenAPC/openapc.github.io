The [University of Milano-Bicocca](https://en.unimib.it/) has updated its cost data, the recent contribution provides APC expenditures for the 2025 period.

Contact Person is [Alessandro Andretto](mailto:alessandro.andretto@unimib.it).

    
## Cost data



The new APC data set covers publication fees for 170 articles, total expenditure amounts to 423,711€ and the average fee is 2,492€.
