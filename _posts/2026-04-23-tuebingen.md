The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen](https://uni-tuebingen.de/en/facilities/university-library/publishing-research/open-access-publication-fund-articles/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Ute Grimmel-Holzwarth](mailto:openaccess@ub.uni-tuebingen.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 603 articles (592 unrelated to any agreement, 11 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 1,618,296€ and the average fee is 2,684€.
