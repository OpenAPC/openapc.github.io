The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University of Siegen](https://www.ub.uni-siegen.de/index.php?id=1510&L=1) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Lena Vinnemann](mailto:oa@ub.uni-siegen.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 33 articles (20 unrelated to any agreement, 13 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 72,498€ and the average fee is 2,197€.
