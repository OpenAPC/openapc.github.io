The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [GFZ German Research Centre for Geosciences](https://www.gfz-potsdam.de/en) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.


## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 112 articles (73 unrelated to any agreement, 39 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 211,696€ and the average fee is 1,890€.
