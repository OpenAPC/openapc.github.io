The [Max Planck Digital Library (MPDL)](https://www.mpdl.mpg.de/en/) has reported its Gold APC expenditures for the 2017 to 2025 period.

MPDL provides [institutional funding for Open Access publications](https://www.mpdl.mpg.de/en/?id=50:open-access-publishing&catid=17:open-access) for researchers affiliated with the [Max Planck Society](http://www.mpg.de/en). MPDL makes use of central agreements with Open Access publishers.

Contact person is [Ádám Dér](mailto:adam.der@maxit.mpg.de).

## Cost data



The new data set covers publication fees for 5,124 articles, total expenditure amounts to 12,393,352€ and the average fee is 2,419€.
