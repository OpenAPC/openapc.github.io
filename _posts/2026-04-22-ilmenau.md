The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Universität Ilmenau](https://www.tu-ilmenau.de/universitaet/quicklinks/universitaetsbibliothek/forschen-publizieren/open-access/open-access-finanzierung/open-access-publikationsfonds) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Lena Strube](mailto:openaccess.ub@tu-ilmenau.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 42 articles (28 unrelated to any agreement, 14 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 92,677€ and the average fee is 2,207€.
