[Chemnitz University of Technology](https://www.tu-chemnitz.de/) has updated its APC expenditures, the latest contribution provides data for the 2019 period.

[Chemnitz University Library](https://www.tu-chemnitz.de/ub/index.html.en) is in charge of the [University's Open Access Publishing Fund](https://www.tu-chemnitz.de/ub/publizieren/openaccess/index.html.en), which received support by the DFG under its [Open-Access Publishing Programme](http://www.dfg.de/en/research_funding/programmes/infrastructure/lis/funding_opportunities/open_access/) until 2019.

Contact person is [Ute Blumtritt](mailto:ute.blumtritt@bibliothek.tu-chemnitz.de).

## Cost data



The new data set covers publication fees for 29 articles. Total expenditure amounts to 36 794€ and the average fee is 1 269€.

The following table shows the payments the Chemnitz University Library has made to publishers in 2019.
