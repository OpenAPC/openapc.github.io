The [University of Milan](http://www.unimi.it/ENG/) has reported its APC expenditures for the first half of 2026.

Contact person is [Paola Galimberti](mailto:paola.galimberti@unimi.it).


## Cost Data



The new APC data set covers publication fees for 227 articles, total expenditure amounts to 613 187€ and the average fee is 2 701€.
