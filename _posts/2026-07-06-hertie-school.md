The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Hertie School](https://www.hertie-school.org/en/research/publishing-and-research-data) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Dr. Christopher Landes](mailto:landes@hertie-school.org).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 3 article (1 unrelated to any agreement, 2 published under an agreement like DEAL).




|                  | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:-----------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|IOP Publishing    |        1|              2500|          2500|
|SAGE Publications |        1|               659|           659|
|Springer Nature   |        1|              1074|          1074|
