The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University of Applied Sciences Mainz](https://www.hs-mainz.de/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.


## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 1 articles (unrelated to any agreement). Total APC expenditure amounts to 2,017€ and the average fee is 2,017€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|                | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:---------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Springer Nature |        1|              2017|          2017|
