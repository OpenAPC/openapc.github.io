The [Leibniz Institute for Solid State and Materials Research Dresden](https://www.ifw-dresden.de/) (IFW) has updated its APC expenditures, providing data for the second half of 2025.

Contact person for the contributed IFW data is [Jana Sonnenstuhl](mailto:j.sonnenstuhl@ifw-dresden.de).

## Cost data





The new data set covers publication fees for 18 articles. Total expenditure amounts to 50,028€ and the average fee is 2,779€.


The following table provides an overview of the new data:



|                                | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:-------------------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Springer Nature                 |        8|             30366|          3796|
|American Physical Society (APS) |        4|              8382|          2095|
|Wiley-Blackwell                 |        4|              8632|          2158|
|Elsevier BV                     |        2|              2648|          1324|
