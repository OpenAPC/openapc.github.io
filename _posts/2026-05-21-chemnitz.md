The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Universität Chemnitz](https://www.tu-chemnitz.de/ub/publizieren/openaccess/index.html.en) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Ute Blumtritt](mailto:Ute.Blumtritt@Bibliothek.TU-Chemnitz.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 79 articles (53 unrelated to any agreement, 26 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 174,465€ and the average fee is 2,208€.
