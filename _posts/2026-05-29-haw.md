The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Hamburg University of Applied Sciences](https://www.haw-hamburg.de/hibs/publizieren/publikationsfonds/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Steffen Rudolph](mailto:hibs.oa@haw-hamburg.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 16 article (5 unrelated to any agreement, 11 published under an agreement like DEAL).

|:------------------------------------------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|

|Elsevier BV                                            |        4|              5463|          1366|
