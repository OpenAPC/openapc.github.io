The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Universität Berlin](http://www.ub.tu-berlin.de/publikationsfonds/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Katharina Drescher](mailto:transformation@ub.tu-berlin.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 195 articles (91 unrelated to any agreement, 104 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 384,729€ and the average fee is 1,973€.
