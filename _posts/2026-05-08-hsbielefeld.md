The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Hochschule Bielefeld - University of Applied Sciences and Arts (HSBI)](https://www.hsbi.de/open-access/publikationsfonds) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Kerstin Werth](mailto:open@hsbi.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 3 articles (2 unrelated to any agreement, 1 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 5,039€ and the average fee is 1,680€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|               | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|

|AIP Publishing |        1|              1636|          1636|
|Elsevier BV    |        1|               753|           753|
|MDPI AG        |        1|              2649|          2649|
