The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Bamberg University](https://www.uni-bamberg.de/en/library/publishing/oa-publishing/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Dr. Fabian Franke](mailto:fabian.franke@uni-bamberg.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 25 articles (12 unrelated to any agreement, 13 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 41,101€ and the average fee is 1,644€.
