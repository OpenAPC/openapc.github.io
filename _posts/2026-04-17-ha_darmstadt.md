The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Darmstadt University of Applied Sciences](https://h-da.de/en/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Katharina Emig](mailto:open-access.bib@h-da.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 11 articles (10 unrelated to any agreement, 1 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 17,891€ and the average fee is 1,626€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|                                                       | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|

|Frontiers Media SA                                     |        4|              7628|          1907|
|Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineers (IEEE) |        3|              3354|          1118|
|MDPI AG                                                |        2|              3336|          1668|
|Institution of Engineering and Technology (IET)        |        1|              1904|          1904|
|Public Library of Science (PLoS)                       |        1|              1669|          1669|
