The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Hochschule Ingolstadt](https://www.thi.de/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.


## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 12 articles (9 unrelated to any agreement, 3 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 27,648€ and the average fee is 2,304€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|                                                       | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:------------------------------------------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|MDPI AG                                                |        5|             13830|          2766|
|Institute of Electrical & Electronics Engineers (IEEE) |        4|              8802|          2201|
|Springer Nature                                        |        2|              2910|          1455|
|Elsevier BV                                            |        1|              2106|          2106|
