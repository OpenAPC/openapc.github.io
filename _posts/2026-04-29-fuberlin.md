The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Freie Universität Berlin](https://www.fu-berlin.de/sites/open_access/finanzierung-oa/zeitschriftenartikel/publikationsfonds_zeitschriften/index.html) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Birgit Schlegel](mailto:birgit.schlegel@fu-berlin.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 146 articles (74 unrelated to any agreement, 72 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 368,919€ and the average fee is 2,527€.
