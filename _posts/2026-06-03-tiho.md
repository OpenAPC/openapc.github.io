The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University of Veterinary Medicine Hannover, Foundation (TiHo)](https://www.tiho-hannover.de/en/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated for both the 2023 and 2024 period.

Contact person is [Hans-Jürgen Osigus](mailto:open.access@tiho-hannover.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 313 articles (246 unrelated to any agreement, 67 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 700 940€ and the average fee is 2 239€.
