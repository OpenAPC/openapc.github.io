The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [GIGA German Institute of Global and Area Studies](https://www.giga-hamburg.de/de/das-giga/giga-bibliothek/open-access) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Jan Lüth](mailto:Jan.Lueth@giga-hamburg.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 1 article (1 unrelated to any agreement, 0 published under an agreement like DEAL).




|               | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:--------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|IOP Publishing |        1|              2600|          2600|
