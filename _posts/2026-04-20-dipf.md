The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Leibniz-Institut für Bildungsforschung und Bildungsinformation (DIPF)](https://www.dipf.de/en/knowledge-resources/dipfdocs-open-access?set_language=en) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Manuela Bielow](mailto:publikationen@dipf.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 4 articles (2 unrelated to any agreement, 2 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 6,856€ and the average fee is 1,714€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|                   | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Springer Nature    |        2|              4383|          2191|
|Frontiers Media SA |        1|              1067|          1067|
|The Royal Society  |        1|              1406|          1406|
