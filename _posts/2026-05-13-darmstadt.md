The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Universität Darmstadt](https://www.ulb.tu-darmstadt.de/forschen_publizieren/publizieren/oa_foerderung/index.de.jsp) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Karen Schiele](mailto:oa-fonds@ulb.tu-darmstadt.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 193 articles (137 unrelated to any agreement, 56 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 484,473€ and the average fee is 2,510€.
