The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Fulda University of Applied Sciences](https://www.hs-fulda.de/en/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact for the data set is to be made via the university's [OA team](mailto:openaccess-hlsb@hs-fulda.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 2 articles (2 unrelated to any agreement, 0 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 5,630€ and the average fee is 2,815€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|                       | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:----------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Georg Thieme Verlag KG |        1|              3630|          3630|
|MDPI AG                |        1|              2000|          2000|
