The [Università degli Studi di Modena e Reggio Emilia](https://www.unimore.it/en) (Unimore) has updated its APC and BPC expenditures, providing data for the 2024-2025 period.

The [Unimore Libraries](https://www.libraries.unimore.it/site/home.html) manage services related to [Open Access](https://www.pop-en.unimore.it/open-access/).

Contact Person is [Andrea Solieri](mailto:andrea.solieri@unimore.it).

## Cost Data



The new data set covers publication fees for 296 articles. Total expenditure amounts to 735,656€ and the average fee is 2,485€. BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) dataset and are not listed here.
