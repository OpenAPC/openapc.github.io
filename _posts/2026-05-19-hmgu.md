The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Helmholtz Zentrum München](https://www.helmholtz-munich.de/en/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Markus Hagemann](mailto:markus.hagemann@helmholtz-muenchen.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 92 article (28 unrelated to any agreement, 64 published under an agreement like DEAL).
