The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Hochschule Bonn-Rhein-Sieg](https://www.h-brs.de/en/bib/publication-fund) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Ulrike Richter](mailto:ulrike.richter@h-brs.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 8 article (6 unrelated to any agreement, 2 published under an agreement like DEAL).
