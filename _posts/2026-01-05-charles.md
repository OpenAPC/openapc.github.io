[Charles University](https://cuni.cz/UKEN-1.html) has provided data on APC and BPC expenditures for the 2024 period.

The [Charles University Library](https://library.cuni.cz/) collects data on Open Access publishing fees paid for by faculties budgets at Charles University and kindly provides this data to OpenAPC.

Contact person is [Yuliia Leshchenko](mailto: openscience@cuni.cz).

## Cost data



## Cost data



The new data set covers publication fees for 468 articles published in 2023. Total expenditure amounts to 1,245,737€ and the average fee is 2,662€. BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) dataset and are not listed here.
