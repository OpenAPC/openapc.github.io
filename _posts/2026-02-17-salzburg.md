The [University of Salzburg](https://www.plus.ac.at/?lang=en) has reported new APC expenditures, the latest contribution provides data for the second half of 2025 funded by the university's publication fund as well as data funded by the Austrian Science Fund (FWF) and non-funded publications for the 2025 period.

The [University Library Salzburg](https://www.plus.ac.at/university-library/?lang=en) manages the university's institutional [Open Access Publication Fund](https://www.plus.ac.at/universitaetsbibliothek/forschen-publizieren/open-access/publikationsfonds/).

Contact Person is [Paul Gredler](mailto:open-access.ubs@plus.ac.at).

## Cost Data



The new data set covers publication fees for 49 articles. Total expenditure amounts to 120,094€ and the average fee is 2,451€.
