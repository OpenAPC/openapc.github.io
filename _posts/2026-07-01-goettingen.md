The [University of Göttingen](https://www.uni-goettingen.de/en/1.html) has updated its expenditures, the latest contribution provides APC and TA data for the 2024 period.

Data is provided by the [Göttingen State and University Library](https://www.sub.uni-goettingen.de/en/), which is in charge of the [University's Open Access Publishing Fund](https://www.sub.uni-goettingen.de/en/publishing/funding-open-access/publication-fund).

Contact person is [Astrid Orth](mailto:oa@sub.uni-goettingen.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 283 article (114 unrelated to any agreement, 169 published under an agreement like DEAL).
