The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University of Education Freiburg](https://www.ph-freiburg.de/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Christian Berger](mailto:openaccess@ph-freiburg.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 10 articles (10 unrelated to any agreement, none published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 14,035€ and the average fee is 1,404€.

The following table provides an overview.  




|                                            | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:-------------------------------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Frontiers Media SA                          |        4|              3495|           874|
|Springer Nature                             |        3|              6267|          2089|
|Elsevier BV                                 |        1|              1233|          1233|
|MDPI AG                                     |        1|              1970|          1970|
|Österreichische Akademie der Wissenschaften |        1|              1071|          1071|
