The [Couperin consortium](https://couperin.org) collects data on APC expenditures from universities and other research institutions in France. 

APC expenditures for 29 different institutions have now been made available to OpenAPC, with five institutions being included for the first time.

Contact person for Couperin is [Valerie Larroque](mailto:apc@couperin.org).

## Cost data



The new data set provided by Couperin covers publication fees for 1,299 articles, total expenditure amounts to 3,522,822€ and the average fee is 2,712€. The data also contained BPC records which are not listed here. BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) data set.
