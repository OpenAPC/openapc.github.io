The [Forschungszentrum Jülich](http://www.fz-juelich.de/portal/DE/Home/home_node.html) collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

Participating institutions may opt-in to provide their data to OpenAPC as well, now Jülich's own expenditures for 2024 have been integrated via the same mechanism.

Contact for the data set is the institution's [Publications team](mailto:zb-copyright@fz-juelich.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 235 articles (88 unrelated to any agreement, 147 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 549,910€ and the average fee is 2,340€.
