The [Couperin consortium](https://couperin.org) collects data on APC expenditures from universities and other research institutions in France. 

APC expenditures for 14 different institutions have now been made available to OpenAPC, with five institutions being included for the first time.

Contact person for Couperin is [Valerie Larroque](mailto:apc@couperin.org).

## Cost data



The new data set provided by Couperin covers publication fees for 1,657 articles, total expenditure amounts to 4,485,951€ and the average fee is 2,707€. The data also contained BPC records which are not listed here. BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) data set.

The data originates from the following institutions:

- Université de Strasbourg
- Université Savoie Mont Blanc
- Université Paris-Saclay - CentraleSupélec - ENS Paris-Saclay (**new**)
- Université Paris Cité
- Université Panthéon-Sorbonne - Paris I
- ONERA - Office National d'Etudes et de Recherches Aérospatiales - The French Aerospace Lab
- Université Lumière - Lyon II
- Université de Lille
- IFREMER - Institut Français de Recherche pour l'Exploitation de la Mer
- ENAC - Ecole Nationale de l'Aviation Civile
- CNRS - Centre national de la recherche scientifique
- CEA - Commissariat à l'énergie atomique et aux énergies alternatives
- Université de Toulouse (**new**)
- Université de Bordeaux 

Please note that the new institutions Université Paris-Saclay and Université de Toulouse have been present in OpenAPC before: 
- Université de Toulouse was previously known as "Université Toulouse III - Paul Sabatier"
- Université Paris-Saclay - CentraleSupélec - ENS Paris-Saclay is a merger of the institutions "CentraleSupélec" and "ENS Paris - Ecole normale supérieure de Paris"

The old names have been dropped from OpenAPC.
