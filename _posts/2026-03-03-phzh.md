The [Pädagogische Hochschule Zürich](https://phzh.ch/en/) (PHZH, Zurich University of Teacher Education) has provided both APC and BPC expenditures for the 2025 period.

The [PHZH Library](https://phzh.ch/de/Dienstleistungen/Bibliothek/) collects data on institutional APC and BPC expenditures and kindly provides this data to OpenAPC.

Contact Person is [Sabina Löhrer](mailto:repositorium@phzh.ch).


## Cost data



The new data set covers publication fees for 2 article. The data also contained 8 BPC records which are not listed here as BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) dataset. 




|                   | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Frontiers Media SA |        2|              4280|          2140|
