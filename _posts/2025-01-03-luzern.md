The [University of Lucerne](https://www.unilu.ch/en/) has updated its expenditures, the latest contribution provides data on APCs and BPCs for the 2023 period.

The [ZHB](https://www.zhbluzern.ch/english-pages/open-science/publishing-open-access) (Central and University Library of Lucerne) collects data on institutional APC and BPC expenditures.

Contact Person is [Simone Rosenkranz](mailto:Simone.Rosenkranz@zhbluzern.ch).


## Cost Data



The new APC data set covers publication fees for 7 articles, total expenditure amounts to 14 948€ and the average fee is 2 135€.

The following table provides an overview. Please note that BPCs are not included in this list, as they are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) data set.




|                              | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:-----------------------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Springer Nature               |        2|              5030|          2515|
|Wiley-Blackwell               |        2|              4921|          2460|
|BMJ                           |        1|              2475|          2475|
|Oxford University Press (OUP) |        1|              2253|          2253|
|Peeters                       |        1|               269|           269|
