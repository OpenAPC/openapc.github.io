The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

Data from the [Eberhard Karls Universität Tübingen](https://uni-tuebingen.de/en/) had not been part of the initial transfer, however, this collection has now been made available to OpenAPC retroactively.

Contact person for the data set is [Ute Grimmel-Holzwarth](mailto:openaccess@ub.uni-tuebingen.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers publication fees for 522 articles, total expenditure amounts to 1,399,393€ and the average fee is 2,681€.
