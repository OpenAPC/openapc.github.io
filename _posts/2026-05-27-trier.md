The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University of Trier](https://www.uni-trier.de/bibliothek/forschen-publizieren/open-access/open-access-publikationsfonds) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Dr. Evgenia Grishina](mailto:openaccess@uni-trier.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 33 articles (27 unrelated to any agreement, 6 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 84,785€ and the average fee is 2,569€.
