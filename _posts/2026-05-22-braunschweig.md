The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Universität Braunschweig](https://ub.tu-braunschweig.de/publikationsfonds/englisch/index_en.php) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Carsten Elsner](mailto:carsten.elsner@tu-braunschweig.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 136 articles (93 unrelated to any agreement, 43 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 261,419€ and the average fee is 1,922€.
