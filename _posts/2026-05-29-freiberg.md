The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [TU Bergakademie Freiberg](https://tu-freiberg.de/en/ub/publishing/open-access) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact Persons are [Martina Obst](mailto:Martina.Obst@ub.tu-freiberg.de) and [Conrad Hübler](mailto:Conrad.Huebler@ub.tu-freiberg.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 33 articles (19 unrelated to any agreement, 14 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 47,162€ and the average fee is 1,429€.
