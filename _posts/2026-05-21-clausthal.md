The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Clausthal University of Technology](https://www.ub.tu-clausthal.de/publizieren-open-access/open-access-publizieren/publikationsfonds) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Silke Frank](mailto:silke.frank@tu-clausthal.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 53 articles (25 unrelated to any agreement, 28 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 125,200€ and the average fee is 2,362€.
