The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Technische Universität Dortmund](https://ub.tu-dortmund.de/forschen-publizieren/open-access/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Dr. Kathrin Höhner](mailto:open-access@tu-dortmund.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 79 articles (44 unrelated to any agreement, 35 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 191,358€ and the average fee is 2,422€.
