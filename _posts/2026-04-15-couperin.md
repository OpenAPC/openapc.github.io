The [Couperin consortium](https://couperin.org) collects data on APC expenditures from universities and other research institutions in France. 

APC expenditures for 2 institutions have now been made available to OpenAPC.

Contact person for Couperin is [Valerie Larroque](mailto:apc@couperin.org).

## Cost data



The new data set provided by Couperin covers publication fees for 1,401 articles, total expenditure amounts to 2,964,335€ and the average fee is 2,116€. The data also contained BPC records which are not listed here. BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) data set.

The data originates from the following institutions:

- CNRS - Centre national de la recherche scientifique
- ONERA - Office National d'Etudes et de Recherches Aérospatiales - The French Aerospace Lab
