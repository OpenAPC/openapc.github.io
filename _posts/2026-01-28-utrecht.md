The [Utrecht University Library](https://www.uu.nl/en/university-library) collects data on APC expenditures from [Utrecht University](https://www.uu.nl/en) and the [University Medical Center Utrecht](https://www.umcutrecht.nl/en). 

A data set on APC expenditures for the 2019 to 2024 period has been kindly made available to OpenAPC.

Contact person is [Jan de Boer](mailto:J.deBoer@uu.nl).

## Cost data



The data set covers publication fees for 3,303 articles, total expenditure amounts to 7,715,219€ and the average fee is 2,336€.
