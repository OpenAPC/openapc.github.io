The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Helmholtz-Zentrum für Umweltforschung – UFZ](https://www.ufz.de/index.php?en=36297) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Paul Meißner](mailto:paul.meissner@ufz.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 149 articles (146 unrelated to any agreement, 3 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 317,014€ and the average fee is 2,128€.
