The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [University of Würzburg](https://www.bibliothek.uni-wuerzburg.de/en/research-publishing/open-access/publication-fund/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Claudia Schober](mailto:openaccess@uni-wuerzburg.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 348 articles (340 unrelated to any agreement, 8 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 926,802€ and the average fee is 2,663€.

The following table provides an overview. Please note that BPCs are not included in this list, as they are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) dataset
