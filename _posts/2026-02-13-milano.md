The [University of Milan](http://www.unimi.it/ENG/) has reported APC expenditures for the second half of 2025 and BPCs for the 2024-2025 period.

Contact person is [Paola Galimberti](mailto:paola.galimberti@unimi.it).


## Cost Data



The new APC data set covers publication fees for 219 articles, total expenditure amounts to 552 736€ and the average fee is 2 524€. BPCs are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) data set and are not listed here.
