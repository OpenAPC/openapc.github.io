The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Alice Salomon Hochschule Berlin](https://www.ash-berlin.eu/studium/einrichtungen-fuer-studierende/bibliothek/open-research/#c41548) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Claudia Voigtländer](mailto:openaccess@ash-berlin.eu).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 1 article (None unrelated to any agreement, 1 published under an agreement like DEAL). 

The following table provides an overview.  




|                | Articles| Fees paid in EURO| Mean Fee paid|
|:---------------|--------:|-----------------:|-------------:|
|Springer Nature |        1|              2327|          2327|
