The Forschungszentrum Jülich collects publication cost data from German institutions as part of the DFG programme "[Open Access Publication Funding](https://www.fz-juelich.de/en/zb/open-science/open-access/monitoring-dfg-oa-publication-funding)".

When reporting their publication costs, the [Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg](https://ub.fau.de/en/research/open-access/) had agreed to share the data with OpenAPC as well. This data has now been transferred and integrated.

Contact person for the data set is [Tonka Stoyanova](mailto:ub-open-access@fau.de).

## Cost data



The new data covers APCs for 407 articles (180 unrelated to any agreement, 227 published under an agreement like DEAL). Total APC expenditure amounts to 897,430€ and the average fee is 2,205€.

The following table provides an overview. Please note that BPCs are not included in this list, as they are aggregated in OpenAPC's [BPC](https://github.com/OpenAPC/openapc-de/blob/master/data/bpc.csv) dataset
